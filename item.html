Items
ML:BB Features a wide variety of items, ranging from boots, to physical attack items, to defense item,to magical items. There's a lot. They are modular and upgradeable enchancements that can be bought for a certain amount of gold in the shop.


Movement Items
Boots. They are really useful in early game for that sweet sweet mobility, there's quite a lot of them, but the most well-known one, is the one, the only, the Krazy Rapid Boots. Idol, hindi porket Gusion ang gamit mo, ay hindi ka na pwede gumamit ng Rapid Boots! But for real, theres quite a lot of them.
  1. Demon Shoes - They are mostly used by heroes that need a sustainable mana supply, it provides +6 mana regen.
  2. Rapid Boots - What can I say? They're rapid boots, they give you extra movement speed when not in battle.
  3. Swift Boots - These boots give you extra attack speed.
  4. Arcane Boots - Provides extra magic penetration.
  5. Magic Shoes - Provides Cooldown reduction.
  6. Tough Boots - Provide magic defense.
  7. Warrior Boots - Provide physical defense.
Defense Items
They're mostly used by tanks as a way to get tankier and beefier and be harder to kill, however, these can be used by every hero.


Attack Items
These items can be used to increase a hero's physical attack capabilities, ranging from crit damage, crit chance, lifesteal, and more. Mostly used on heroes with physical attacks.


Magic Items
These items can be used to increase a hero's magical capabilities, magic damage, magic lifesteal, and more. Mostly used on heroes with magical attacks.
