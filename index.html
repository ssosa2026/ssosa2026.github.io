Mechanics
Mobile Legends: Bang Bang (MLBB) Is a 5v5 Competitive MOBA Game, wherein you take control of a hero to defeat enemies, level up, get stronger, get richer, get more items, and in the end, destroy the enemy team to win the game. The game has 4 main gamemodes, Classic, Ranked, Brawl, and Arcade. Classic is the basic gameplay experience, wherein you choose your hero, and get pitted up with 4 other teammates, and go against a team of 5 enemies in a map with 3 lanes. In ranked, it is basically the same, however it is more competitive and has a ranking system. There are eight ranks in the game, "Warrior" is the lowest, followed by "Elite", then "Master", "Grandmaster", "Epic", "Legend", "Mythic", and finally, "Mythical Glory", the highest rank.




Brawl is another gamemode that is available in ML:BB, Brawl differs a little from the basic gameplay. Only having 1 lane, and only being able to buy items at base.




Arcade is a gamemode in ML that hosts other mini-gamemodes such as Magic Chess, and other time-limited ones like Mirror, Mayhem, and Hyper Blend.
