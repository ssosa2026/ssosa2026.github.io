Map and Jungle
ML:BB takes place in a 3-lane map, in a diagonal orientation, there's the top lane, the mid lane, and the bot lane. The top and bot lane can either be the EXP, or the Gold Lane. There is also the jungle, which are the parts that are not the lanes on your team, nor the enemy team's side.

The jungle contains many jungle creeps, some of which provide special buffs after being slain. Heroes that are equipped with the retribution spell and has jungling equipment are significantly faster at clearing said jungle creeps, some heroes are also more suited to jungle compared to other heroes, these heroes are mostly fighters and assassins.

These buffs provide different effects, the purple buff provides reduced energy and mana cost for skills, cooldown reduction, and different amounts of HP depending on what creep or enemy you've slain.
